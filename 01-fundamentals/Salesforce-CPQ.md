## What is Salesforce CPQ?
Salesforce CPQ stands for Configure, Price, Quote, designed to automate and streamline the creation of accurate, customized quotes for complex products and services. It enables sales representatives to quickly generate quotes by configuring products based on predefined rules, applying correct pricing and discounts, and ensuring compliance with business policies, all within the Salesforce environment.

## Key Problems it Solves
- Manual quote errors
- Complex pricing logic
- Approval delays

## Core CPQ Components
- Products
- Price Books
- Price Rules
- Bundles
- Discount Schedules

## Real-world Example
A telecom company offering internet + OTT bundles with volume-based discounts.
